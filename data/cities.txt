Paris is the capital of France. It is famous for the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and Seine River cruises. 
The best time to visit is April to June and September to November. 
Local foods include croissants, baguettes, escargot, and French wine.

New York City is located in the USA. It is known for Times Square, Central Park, Statue of Liberty, and Broadway shows. 
The best time to visit is April to June and September to early November. 
Local foods include pizza, burgers, and cheesecakes.

Tokyo is the capital of Japan. It is famous for Shibuya Crossing, Tokyo Tower, Senso-ji Temple, and anime culture. 
The best time to visit is March to May and October to November. 
Local foods include sushi, ramen, and tempura.

Dubai is located in the UAE. It is famous for Burj Khalifa, desert safari, luxury shopping malls, and beaches. 
The best time to visit is November to March. 
Popular experiences include dune bashing and yacht cruises.

Rome is the capital of Italy. It is famous for the Colosseum, Roman Forum, Vatican City, and ancient architecture. 
The best time to visit is April to June and September to October. 
Local foods include pasta, pizza, and gelato.
